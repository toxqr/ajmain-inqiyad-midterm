Project Overview

An AI-assisted redesign of Harlem plaza focused on walking, safety, and local business activity.

Existing Conditions

The current plaza feels underused and lacks strong reasons for people to stay, move through, or explore.

Core Problem

Low pedestrian engagement, unclear circulation, and weak connections to nearby businesses reduce plaza activity.

User Groups

The system is designed for pedestrians, local businesses, and city planners who all benefit from a more active plaza.

Site Analysis

The site study examines walking routes, comfort, safety, visibility, and areas with the most activity potential.
